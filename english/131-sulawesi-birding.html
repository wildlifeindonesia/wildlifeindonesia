SULAWESI BIRDING

Sulawesi is a big island in Indonesia. It is located in the transitional zone between the continent of Asia and Australia. This makes the avifauna of Sulawesi unique. Alfred Russel Wallace devides this region by tracing a line that runs between Borneo (Kalimantan) and Celebes (Sulawesi) and then through Lombok strait between Bali and Lombok island. According to the website of Avibase - The World Bird Database, in Sulawesi and its satellite islands, there are 519 species of birds with 89 of them are endemics.

There are a lot of birding sites in Sulawesi especially in the North and Central regions. Minahasa Peninsula is a must visit destination for birding visitors. For highland birds, visitors can go to Mount Mahawu and Subdistrict Sonder.
