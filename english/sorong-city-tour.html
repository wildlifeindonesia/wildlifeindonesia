Birdwatching and Sightseeing Tour in Sorong City

Sorong city is the main entrance gate for visitors who want to visit Raja Ampat islands. It is also the capital of the newly formed province of Papua Barat Daya.

Sorong city itself has got a number of tourist attractions. As a tourist guide, I often run tours for visitors to some interesting places that I describe below:

  • Mangrove Forest - This is a great place for visitors who want to see the ecosystem of mangrove forest and also do birdwatching and wildlife watching. Birds that can be seen in this forest includes: Brown-backed Honeyeater, Olive-backed Sunbird, Orange-fronted Fruit Dove, White-bellied Cuckooshrike, Sacred Kingfisher, Blue-fronted Figparrot, Common Sandpiper, Striated Heron, Glossy Swiftlet, Moustached Treeswift, and etc. When I guide tourists to this place, I usually bring a pair of binoculars that is mounted on a tripod.   • Natural Tourism Park/ Protected Forest - Local people usually call it Hutan Lindung meaning protected forest. Visitors can enjoy wildlife watching in this forest. There are various kinds of plantd and animals which tourists can see while visiting this forest.
  • Sorong has got a number of buildings whose designs are unique. One of them is The Cathedral Church. Its architectural design is inspired by the bower of the Vogelkop Bowerbird.
