Birding in West Papua of Indonesia

West Papua is a province in eastern region of Indonesia. It has become a favorite destination for birding visitors who want to see birds of paradise, parrots, pigeons and doves as well as kingfishers, nightjars, cassowary, and pittas.

There are a lot of birding sites in West Papua. Some of them are listed below:

  • Manado City Tours
  • Birdwatching in Sonder town of Minahasa
  • Birding in Minahasa
  • Traveling in North Sulawesi
  • Holiday in the Highland of Minahasa
  • Birdwatching and Wildlife Watching Tour in Sulawesi
