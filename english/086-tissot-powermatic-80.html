Silicium

Silicium or silicon semiconductor material that is often used in the manufacturing of various components in watches. This allows them to be more resistant to magnetic fields emitted by cell phones, laptops, desktops, radios, electrical machines, doors of refrigerator, hair dryer, loud speakers, closure buttons of hand-bags, and etc. The use of silicon in tiny parts of the wrist watch greatly increases the accuracy of the watch.
