Sorong city in Papua Barat Daya province is the main gate to Raja Ampat. Most tourists who want to enjoy snorkeling, scuba diving, birding and sighseeing in Raja Ampat regency fly to Domine Eduard Osok airport of Sorong and then continue their trip by passenger ferry to Waisai town. In general, tourists spend one or two days waiting for their return flights or waiting for their liveaboard dive boats in Sorong city. During this time, they look for activities that they can do.

Sightseeing

For this purpose, I offer Hiking Tour in Sorong Ridge Forest. During this tour, participants will be able to see the landscape view of Tropical Rainforest of Sorong city. Please, wear hiking boots or sport shoes. I recommend that participants bring a digital camera. Participants of the tour will also see mountains that are covered by rainforest. The rainforest itself is the natural habitat of birds, butterflies, beetles, and a lot of unique tropical plants. After hiking along the ridge, visitors can enjoy swimming in the natural pool at the foot of a small waterfall. Don't forget to bring bathing suit. While participants swim in the river, I and one or two local guides will prepare hot water for making tea and coffee.

Birding

Visitors who bring binoculars and spotting scope can also watch such birds as:

  • Pinon Imperial Pigeon
  • Orange-bellied Fruit Dove
  • Yellow-faced Myna
  • Singing Starling
  • Oriental Dollarbird
  • Rainbow Bee-eater
  • Brahminy Kite
  • Grey-headed Goshawk
  • Sulphur-crested Cockatoo
  • Palm Cockatoo
  • Eclectus Parrot
  • Double-eyed Fig Parrot
  • Black-capped Lory
  • Black Lory
  • Coconut Lorikeet (formerly called Rainbow Lorikeet)
  • Fairy lorikeet
  • Olive-backed Sunbird
  • Black Sunbird
  • Black-browed Triller
  • Olive-crowned Flowerpecker
  • and etc.

Architectural Attractions

There are some buildings whose architectural characteristic are interesting and can be visited by tourists.

  • The Cathedral Church of Christ the King - The design of this church was inspired by the structure of nest of Vogelkop Bowerbird (Amblyornis inornatus). Vogelkop Bowerbird attracts his female counterpart by decorating his bower with colorful flowers, beetle wings and etc.
  • Another interesting building in Sorong city is the Buddhist Temple. It was built on a hill overlooking the beautiful view of Sorong harbor, its blue sea and Doom island.
